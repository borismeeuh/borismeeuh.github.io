General introduction

My name is Boris Kamstra. I'm nineteen years old. I'm from Stellendam, a tiny village about an hour north from Middelburg.

Stellendam on a map


In high school, I attended an Informatica class. One of the things we had to do was to build a basic website. My experience with programming languages is fairly limited, but I really liked what we had to do. That's the main reason I ended up choosing an ICT course. I chose HZ because I really like Middelburg. It's not too small, but not too big and crowded either. In my Informatica class, I got to experience the very basics of programming and I really enjoyed it. That is the primary reason why I believe the ICT course suits me well.
