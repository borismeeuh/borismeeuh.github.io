Macaw



Macaw is a company that functions mainly within Europe. Their employees are mainly from the Netherlands, Germany and Lithuania. T hey pride themselves in the quality that they deliver. Macaw goes the extra mile to satisfy their costumers, which is why they have built such a good name for themselves. They strive to make their company a comfortable place to work too. Macaw cares a lot about their employees, which is why their ‘offices’ are often described as ‘living rooms’. A few of their IT roles include Data Engineer, Business Consultant Data and Analytics, Client Consultant, Creative Consultant and Security consultant.

Macaw always uses the latest technology that’s available, even if it’s not yet available to the public on the market. This gives them an edge over their competition. This draws many new clients to them, since there are not many companies that can say the same thing.

Veracode



Veracode is a company that specialises in building application security programmes. Considering the fact that technology is playing an ever growing role in our lives, companies like Veracode are very important. Veracode makes sure that the code for your company is safely stored and can safely be expanded upon. The company has been around for over a decade now, so they’ve got quite a bit of experience. Hence their good reputation in the industry.

Veracode is a big company, therefore they’ve got a lot of different vacancies. These include, but are not limited to Account Executive, Global Marketing Campaign Manager, Senior Customer Success Manager, Security Program Manager and Marketing Operations Specialist.

Onyx Cybersecurity



Onyx Cybersecurity offers practical help with cybersecurity and privacy issues, for all companies and government. They work with a team of certified specialists in the field of technical security, compliance, process management and privacy legislation. Onix’ team consists of a group of permanent employees, who work according to the agile scrum method. Their employees excel in different fields and work together effectively. I couldn’t find any vacancies for Onyx on their website or LinkedIn.

Mentormate



Mentormate is a company that focuses on web development. They’ve got headquarters in the USA, Bulgaria and Sweden. Mentormate builds, for example, enterprise platforms, responsive web apps and marketing sites. Their developers deliver consistent, high quality products. Their employees are familiar with a wide range of programming and scripting languages and are dedicated to exceed the expectations of the costumer.

There are quite a few different vacancies for Mentormate. These include Account Management, Operations Manager, Cloud and DevOps Manager, Mobile Development and Consulting.

Treyarch



Treyarch is one of the biggest game development studios out there right now. They are best known for their incredibly popular ‘Call of Duty: Black Ops’ series. They’ve got a big team with people that specialize in different areas of game development. Treyarch has been around since 1996, so over the years, they’ve obtained a huge amount of experience. Not only that, but most employees are gamers themselves, so they look at the game from a developer as well as a player aspect.

There is much more to creating a game than writing the code for said game. That’s why Treyarch has entire departments dedicated to things like Design, Engineering and Business and Marketing. Some of their vacancies are Principal Graphics Engineer, Producer, Weapons Artist, Character Artist and Associate Level Designer.
